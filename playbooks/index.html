What these playbooks will be

Each playbook is a short, prioritized hunt sequence: what to check first, what distinguishes this actor or scenario from noise, and links into the NET and HOST references for the actual detection queries. Not a report. Not a technique list. A checklist you open when you need to know where to start.

Playbooks are hand-written from operational experience, not auto-generated from MITRE mappings. The value is in the prioritization and the distinctive tells, not the technique coverage.
